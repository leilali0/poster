Helvetica®
1957

Refinement of charactors
A number of charactors were subbly changed to be more consistent and more harmonious wiht the overall design, as well as the improve legibility.

New numbering system
Each weight is identified by a number in addition to the weight name - for easy reference, similar to the Univers® and Frutiger® design convertions.

Cap and x-height adjustment
The cap height is now consisten, correcting subtle differnces and the x-height has been adjusted to appear visually the same in all weights.

The original design, drawn by Max Miedinger, was released by the Haas Type Foundry of Switzerland, then by Germany-based Stempel (the something company of Hass) and xxxx by Mergenthaler Linotype. In 1983, Stempel released Neue Helvetica, a re-working of hte design. Sinve its luanch, Helvetica has been refined by a variety of designers to add new weigts and adapt the typeface for successive methods of composition, from xxxxetal to digital.
